
# Masjid Tua-Game

<img width="1452" height="816" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ebdf5354-5cd2-4fa6-bd46-886487b187a2" />

Masjid Tua-Game is a first-person horror adventure game developed using Unity and C#. Players take the role of a young santri tasked with restoring the sanctity of an abandoned mosque that has been overtaken by jinn and shrouded in darkness.

The game combines exploration, resource management, and survival horror elements. Players must search for scattered Qur'an manuscripts, manage limited flashlight batteries, survive supernatural encounters, and ultimately perform the adzan to cleanse the mosque.

## Story

A once-sacred mosque has been abandoned for years and transformed into a dark labyrinth inhabited by jinn. As a young santri, the player is assigned a sacred mission: recover the lost Qur'an manuscripts scattered throughout the mosque and return them to their rightful place.

As the mission progresses, the supernatural presence within the mosque becomes increasingly aggressive. Only after all manuscripts have been restored can the player reach the Imam Room and perform the final adzan to drive away the jinn and restore peace.

---

## Gameplay Objective

The player's objective is to:

- Explore the abandoned mosque
- Locate all lost manuscripts
- Collect flashlight batteries
- Survive encounters with jinn
- Return manuscripts to the main bookshelf
- Reach the Imam Room
- Perform the final adzan
